About Research

Christopher Jung

csj93 at stanford.edu

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Stanford University, hosted by Omer Reingold. Previously, I completed my PhD at UPenn where I was fortunate to be advised by Aaron Roth and Michael Kearns. Before Penn, I studied computer science at Cornell University (Summa Cum Laude with Honors) and worked as a software engineer at APT(Applied Predictive Technologies) which is now part of MasterCard.
